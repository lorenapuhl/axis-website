Create a modern SaaS-style “Features Section” for a landing page FeatureSection.tsx

This section is for a startup that builds high-converting websites for fitness studios (pilates, yoga, dance, boxing, etc.). The product turns an Instagram-based studio into a complete booking + payment + management system.

The design must feel like a premium, minimal SaaS product (inspired by Stripe / Apple / Notion). Clean, structured, and instantly understandable in under 2 seconds.

---

## SECTION STRUCTURE

1. Headline (centered)
2. Subheadline (centered)
3. Feature Grid (main focus)
4. Technical Features Strip (secondary, below)

---

## HEADLINE + SUBHEADLINE

Headline:
“Everything your studio needs to grow”

Subheadline:
“Bookings. Payments. Clients. All in one simple system.”

Typography:

* Large, bold headline
* Smaller, muted subheadline
* Tight spacing, modern feel

---

## FEATURE GRID (CORE OF SECTION)

### Layout

* Responsive grid
* Desktop: 3 columns × 2 rows
* 6 cards total
* First row: 2 larger “hero” cards + 1 normal card
* Second row: 3 normal cards

Grid example:

[ LARGE ][ LARGE ][ SMALL ]
[ SMALL ][ SMALL ][ SMALL ]

Spacing:

* Generous padding
* Equal gaps between cards
* Clean alignment

---

## CARD DESIGN SYSTEM

Each card must include:

1. Minimal icon (top-left)
2. Title (bold, 1 line)
3. Short outcome sentence (max 1 line)
4. Small UI fragment (visual proof)

Card style:

* Rounded corners (soft, modern)
* Subtle border
* Light shadow
* Neutral background (white or off-white)

Hover interaction:

* Slight lift (translateY)
* Shadow increases
* UI fragment becomes slightly sharper or brighter

## IMPORTANT DESIGN RULES

* Keep text extremely short and scannable
* No paragraphs inside cards
* UI fragments must be simplified, not detailed
* Everything should feel consistent and cohesive
* Focus on clarity over decoration

---

## TECHNICAL FEATURES STRIP (SECONDARY SECTION)

Place directly below the grid.

Purpose:
Show that everything else is handled, without cluttering the main message.

---

### Title (small, subtle):

“Everything else is already handled”

---

### Layout:

* Horizontal list OR 2-row mini grid
* Each item includes:

  * small icon
  * short label (1 line)

---

### FEATURES TO INCLUDE

* Your own domain (yourstudio.com)
* Mobile-optimized
* Built-in analytics
* SEO-ready
* Secure hosting
* Fast performance
* Contact integrations (WhatsApp / email)
* Easy setup (no tech skills needed)

---

### STYLE

* Smaller than feature grid
* Lower visual hierarchy
* Muted colors
* Clean and minimal

---

### MICROCOPY ABOVE OR BELOW

Add a small trust line:

“No setup. No maintenance. No tech headaches.”

---

## FINAL DESIGN INTENT

The entire section should communicate instantly:

* This is a complete system
* It increases bookings and revenue
* It replaces Instagram DMs and manual work
* It is simple and automated
* It is real (via UI fragments)

Avoid:

* clutter
* long text
* complex UI
* repeated animations

Focus on:
clarity, structure, and perceived product quality.

---

Make the design feel like a real SaaS product, not a marketing page.
