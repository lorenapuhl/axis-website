About

Most studios I talk to are dealing with the same thing:
a constant flow of Instagram DMs, repeated questions about schedules and pricing, and bookings managed manually.

It works — but it doesn’t scale.

I noticed that many studios are incredible at building a presence on Instagram, but they’re running their entire business through it.
That means missed bookings, lost clients from Google, and hours spent on admin every week.

So I started building simple systems that turn that activity into something more structured:
a clean, professional website where clients can book classes, buy memberships, and find everything they need — without back-and-forth messages.

My background is in data science and automation, where I’ve built systems designed to be reliable, efficient, and easy to use.
I apply that same thinking here: remove friction, reduce manual work, and let the system run in the background.

I also believe your website shouldn’t become another thing you have to manage.
That’s why it automatically updates using your Instagram content — so it stays fresh without extra effort.

This is built specifically for pilates, yoga, dance, and boutique fitness studios that already have demand — but need a better way to manage it.

The goal is simple:
help you look more professional, get discovered outside Instagram, and turn more visitors into paying clients — without adding more work to your day.
