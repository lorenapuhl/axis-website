I. Instagram Automation
------------------------------------

- Q: Do I need to manually upload my Instagram posts to my website?

A: No. Our system syncs with your account in real-time. Every time you post a new Reel or photo on Instagram, it automatically appears on your website’s gallery. It keeps your site looking fresh and active without you ever having to log in to an editor.


- Q: What if I post something personal on my Instagram?

A: You have full control. You can set the automation to pull from specific hashtags or simply display your latest feed. Your website remains a professional reflection of your studio, powered by the content you’re already creating.

- Q: Can I control which Instagram posts appear in different sections of my site?

A: Absolutely. You have total creative control using simple hashtags. By adding a tag like #news, #events, or #promo to your Instagram caption, our system automatically routes that post to the corresponding column on your website. It’s the easiest way to update your "Announcements" or "Special Offers" page without ever leaving the Instagram app.

- Q: I’m not a "tech person." Is this hard to use?

A: If you can post on Instagram , you can use this. We handle the heavy lifting of the setup so you can focus on your clients.

- Q: I’m already busy with social media. Will this be more work?

A: Actually, it saves you work. Instead of creating content for Instagram and then updating a website, you just keep doing what you’re already doing on Instagram. Your website uses that same content to show prospective clients what your classes actually look like.

- Q: Does Instagram content help me show up on Google?

A: Yes. When your website is "active" with fresh content, Google views it as a high-quality, relevant site. By automatically pulling in your Instagram posts, your website stays "alive" in the eyes of search engines, helping you rank higher than studios with static, dead websites.

- Q: Why not just let people look at my Instagram directly?

A: Instagram is great for browsing, but it's a "noisy" environment with many distractions. Your website takes that same beautiful content and places it right next to a "Book Now" button. We turn your followers' curiosity into confirmed revenue in just two clicks.
