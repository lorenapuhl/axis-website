I built this because I watched it happen over and over.

I'm Lorena — a data scientist and ML engineer. I spent years building systems for embassies, insurance companies, and research labs. But the problem that stuck with me wasn't corporate. It was personal.

The best studios I knew — the ones with incredible coaches, loyal clients, and real community — were completely invisible online. Fully booked through DMs. Losing clients to Google searches they'd never show up in. Spending Sunday nights manually coordinating class schedules in WhatsApp.

"Your studio is already working. Your website just needs to catch up."

So I built AutoSync — not as a generic website builder, but as a system designed specifically for how fitness studios actually run: Instagram-first, owner-operated, and always short on time.

I bring the same rigor I applied to 60GB research pipelines and AI automation at diplomatic offices — now focused entirely on one problem: helping studio owners stop losing revenue to a gap their Instagram can't fill.

Background in machine learning, data engineering, and full-stack development. Based in Mexico City. Fluent in English, Spanish, German and French.
