## Layout

Design and implement a **high-converting SaaS pricing + trust + process section** for a modern startup that builds booking-enabled websites for fitness and dance studios.

The design must feel:

* Premium
* Minimal
* Human
* High-trust
* Not like a mass SaaS product

Do NOT specify font families.

* Reduce friction to near zero
* Make next steps extremely clear
* Create a sense of **exclusivity and limited access**
